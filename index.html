ME. MYSELF & I:

I’m the kind of person who has always found a strange sense of calm in the middle of a storm. I’ve felt it in the heat of a chaotic kitchen, and I’ve been that steady voice for someone whose business, and maybe a piece of their world, just collapsed. My whole career has been about finding clarity when everything feels like it's falling apart.

My journey to becoming a developer wasn't a straight line, but it’s the twists and turns that have made all the difference. Those intense moments in the kitchen taught me how to be precise and focused, even when it felt like everything was burning down. Working in IT, I learned to listen beyond the panic in someone's voice to gently uncover the real problem underneath.

Now, I bring all of that heart and experience into creating applications that just feel right, the kind that you can depend on. The joy I get from launching a seamless app is the same warmth I felt when I could tell a frantic client that everything was going to be okay. It’s all about creating things that genuinely help people, that give them a little bit of their precious time and peace of mind back.

If you’re facing a challenge that feels overwhelming and could use a fresh set of eyes, I’d truly love to connect. I’ve always believed that some of the most wonderful solutions start with nothing more than a good cup of coffee and a real conversation.
